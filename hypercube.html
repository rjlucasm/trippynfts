Hypercube

Concentric squares are widely explored in the artistic world, taking as an example the works of Frank Stella and Emmanuel Moses, which are our inspirations for this project.

Combining this geometry with audio visualization, we believe it's a perfect fit. It's simple and enigmatic.

The musical style of this project is mainly Space Music, which is a genre more focused on ambient music.

We believe it creates this strange, dark atmosphere and makes the concentric squares kind of hypnotic as the music frequency pulsates.

We hope you enjoy :)
